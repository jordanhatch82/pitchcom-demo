⚾ Call pitches from your phone.

They show up on your players' Apple Watches — instantly.

Hand signals get missed, stolen, and slow everything down. Instead: you tap the call on your phone, and a half-second later the right players feel a buzz on their wrist and see exactly what's coming — in a display made for where they play.

A couple of honest notes: it doesn't decide whether your league allows devices on the field — that's the coaches' call. And it's not a scorekeeper; it just sends the calls. The watches connect over cell or a hotspot you bring; it only struggles where there's truly no signal at all.
